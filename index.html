About Synee

Synee is your go-to app for discovering, creating, and attending local events tailored to your interests. Whether you're looking for community gatherings, exploring new local hotspots, or seeking genuine social connections to overcome loneliness, Synee keeps you connected and engaged.

Explore

Synee makes it easy to explore events shared by others and discover what’s happening around you. Search by topic to find gatherings that match your interests or browse nearby events for spontaneous plans. Our interactive map helps you navigate local happenings effortlessly, so you can step into new experiences, meet like-minded people, and make the most of your city.

Create

Unleash your creativity and design your own local events. With Synee, you take the lead in uniting people around shared interests and crafting unforgettable experiences. Whether you are hosting a private gathering or a dynamic social meetup, our platform empowers you to spark genuine connections and create moments worth remembering.

Attend

Found an event that sparks your interest? Don’t just scroll—join in and experience it in real life! Every public event lets you RSVP with a single tap, but spots fill up fast, so act quickly. Stay connected before, during, and after the event by engaging with fellow attendees, sharing thoughts, and posting photos or videos to capture the moment.

How Does it Work?

Sign-Up

Getting started with Synee is quick and effortless. Sign up in just a minute by choosing a username and adding a few details about yourself. To ensure a safe and authentic community, the app is exclusively for users aged 18 and older.

Explore

Once you've created and verified your account, you're all set to dive in. Start exploring events in your local area right away and discover what’s happening around you. Seamlessly switch between Map and List View to find the perfect event.

Engage

Join events that interest you with a simple tap. Not ready to commit? Follow the event to save it for later and stay updated. But don’t wait too long—once the event reaches capacity, spots will no longer be available!

Attend

When the time comes, just attend the event—it’s that easy! It’s normal to feel nervous, but remember, everyone is likely feeling the same way. You’re there to connect, enjoy, and maybe even make new friends. Overcoming that initial hesitation is the first step to meaningful real-life connections.
